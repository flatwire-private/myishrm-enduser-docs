## â“ Common Questions

**Q: Can I edit an invoice after generating it?**
A: No, invoices cannot be edited once generated. Contact your admin if there's an error.

**Q: What if applicant doesn't pay?**
A: You can see overdue invoices. Follow up with applicant via their contact information.

**Q: Can I convert applicant to student?**
A: No, only the [Registrar](registrar.md) can convert applicants after payment is verified.

**Q: What if documents are unclear?**
A: Contact the applicant and ask them to re-upload clear documents before approving.
